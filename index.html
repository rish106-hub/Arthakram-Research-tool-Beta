// The Framework

7 steps from chaos
to clarity

Every problem gets the same rigorous treatment. No shortcuts, no surface-level analysis — just structured thinking that wins.

01
Strip the Core Problem
Removes noise. Rewrites the problem in one sentence. Identifies who, why now, and what changes if solved.
02
First Principles Deconstruction
Finds atomic value questions. Audits assumptions. Identifies the riskiest bet that could collapse your entire strategy.
03
Business Anatomy
Maps stakeholders, constraints, and success definitions. Separates what's non-negotiable from what's a variable.
04
Derived Research Questions
Specific, falsifiable questions traceable to the core problem — the ones competitor teams will miss.
05
Perplexity Prompt
A ready-to-copy research brief built for Perplexity AI. Benchmarks, failure patterns, customer psychology — all requested specifically.
06
Research Angles
10–12 critical questions tagged [MARKET] [CUSTOMER] [FINANCIAL] [STRATEGIC] [OPERATIONAL] [RISK].
07
Working Hypotheses
3 testable claims before research begins. "We believe X because Y. This would be false if Z." Start researching with direction.
